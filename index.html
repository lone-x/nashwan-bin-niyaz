Profile & Ethos

Curious by default.
Engineer
by training.

I'm Nashwan — a software engineer from Kerala with a habit of going wherever curiosity points. I started with HTML and CSS, picked up JavaScript, got into WordPress, moved into Laravel backends, tried Flutter for mobile, dabbled in robotics with Arduino, and ended up training machine learning models. No grand plan. Just an inability to stop learning.

I spent 9 months as an Associate Software Engineer at Axel Technologies, building a live SaaS CRM — REST APIs, role-based auth, background queues, large-scale exports. Before that, internships in Flutter development and robotics. The common thread isn't a stack. It's that I pick things up fast and build something real with them.

"Curiosity has occasionally gotten me in trouble. But even when it has, it's been interesting trouble." — Brian Grazer
